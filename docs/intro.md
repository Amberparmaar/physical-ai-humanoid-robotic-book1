## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that interact directly with the physical world. Unlike traditional AI that processes data in abstract digital environments, Physical AI systems must navigate, manipulate objects, and adapt to the unpredictable nature of physical environments. This course will explore how AI can be embodied in robotic systems to perform complex tasks, from simple navigation to sophisticated manipulation tasks.

## The Rise of Humanoid Robotics

Humanoid robots represent one of the most ambitious frontiers in robotics. With human-like form and capabilities, these robots promise unprecedented interaction potential with human-centered environments. From assistive robots in homes to collaborative robots in workplaces, humanoid robotics is poised to transform how we interact with machines.
